Set in a wasteland, three teams team up or fight!

ModBlockTri is a Minecraft modpack where you play in a wasteland map with three "forest islands" where three players/teams can build a base and team up and cooperate or fight eachother. Heavily inspired by FTB Trident.

Suggestions, issues or something else? Make an issue on GitHub(use the ModBlockTri template) or send an email.
