Forge dropping support, launchers and ModBlock 3

Author: TechnicProblem

2020-01-02

Hi. I haven't done much to the modpack for quite some time now, but I want to write about my plans for 1.14/1.15.
A couple of days ago, the Forge team announced that they are dropping support for 1.12 and 1.14 will turn into a LTS version. Since 1.14 and 1.15 doesn't have the amount of mods that 1.12 has, ModBlock will continue to be developed for 1.12 until there are enough mods on 1.14/1.15 to remake the pack. A "ModBlock 3" will be made for 1.14 or 1.15, depending on what version is going to be supported.

With the FTB team leaving Twitch, they're making a new launcher. As part of this redesign we do plan to add in support for 3rd party modpack developers to distribute their packs through our new app- slowpoke101, FTB. I will continue to have ModBlock on CurseForge as long as I can, and if possible also have it on FTB if it will work. I have no idea how they're going to do it.

Thanks for playing ModBlock, and happy new year!
