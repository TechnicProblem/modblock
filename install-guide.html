A custom lightweight kitchen-sink Minecraft modpack for 1.12.2.

Installing the modpack

Open the Twitch App and click on mods in the top navigation. Click on Minecraft. Then click on Browse All Modpacks.

Search for ModBlock.


Click on Install.


Go back to the modpack menu and click on Play. Done!
