A custom lightweight kitchen-sink Minecraft modpack for 1.12.2.

How to disable a mod

1. Click on the modpack logo in the Twitch launcher.
2. Click on the ... in the top right corner and click on Profile Options.
3. Uncheck the "Locked" box.
4. Deactive the mod by clicking on the switch next to the mod.
